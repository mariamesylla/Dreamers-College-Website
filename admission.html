Overview

Our academic program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business fundamentals, while also allowing them to explore their interests and develop specialized skills in areas such as marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship. Our courses are taught by experienced faculty members who bring a wealth of real-world knowledge and expertise to the classroom.

Courses

Our curriculum includes a variety of courses that cover topics such as:

  • Introduction to Business
  • Marketing
  • Financial Accounting
  • Managerial Accounting
  • Business Law
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Business Plan Development
  • International Business
  • Strategic Management

Students also have the opportunity to take elective courses in areas such as:

  • Digital Marketing
  • Investment Analysis
  • Leadership
  • Social Entrepreneurship
  • E-commerce
  • Project Management

Faculty

Our faculty members are experts in their fields, with many years of experience in business and academia. They are committed to providing students with a high-quality education that prepares them for success in the business world. Some of our faculty members include:

Resources

In addition to our faculty and courses, we offer a variety of resources to help students succeed, including:

  • The Business Library, which houses a collection of books, journals, and other resources on business and entrepreneurship
  • The Business Incubator, which provides students with workspace, mentorship, and other resources to help them develop their business ideas
  • The Career Center, which offers career counseling, job search assistance, and networking opportunities with alumni and business leaders
  • The Student Business Association, which provides students with opportunities to connect with other business students and participate in business-related activities and events
